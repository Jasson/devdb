添加好友的附加消息


C1要添加C2为好友
C1 = user1@91youbao.com
C2 = user2@91youbao.com

C1 -> S 发送添加好朋友请求
<presence type='subscribe' to ='user2@91youbao.com'>
  <status>添加好友的附加消息</status>
</presence>


S -> C2 接收到的添加好友请求
<presence xmlns="jabber:client" 
  from="user1@91youbao.com" to="user2@91youbao.com" type="subscribe">
  <status>添加好友的附加消息</status>
</presence>

这些附加消息在ejabberd是存储在rosterusers表的askmessage字段.
